Welcome to EngineerKappa's River!

Hi there! I'm Anna, also known as EngineerKappa, and occasionally QueenDelta.

I'm an artist, game developer, and all around creative weirdo that's still trying to decide what she wants to do with her life. I'm just doing what I can, and that leads me down all sorts of wacky creative rabbitholes. I don't expect many people to come by, so this is just my own little hub of the various creative projects I've done (or started) over the years.

You can use the navigation bar at the top to check out different projects of mine. Some pages might even have their own subpages, so keep your eyes peeled!
