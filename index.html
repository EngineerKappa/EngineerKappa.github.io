Welcome to EngineerKappa's River!

Hi there! I'm Anna, also known as EngineerKappa, and occasionally QueenDelta.

I'm an artist, a game developer, and an all around creative weirdo that doesn't know how to focus on a single thing. I'm just doing what I can, and that leads me down all sorts of wacky creative rabbitholes. This is my little hub to gather my thoughts, talk about the things I've made, share things I like, and really just carving a small space out for myself on an increasingly hostile corporate-owned internet.

The navigation bar at the top has some of the more important pages, but I've got a few subpages lying around too.

Site Updates

09/17/24 - Undergoing a HEAVY rework of the website! Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • The site's now on http://engineerkappa.net/! I decided to move from Neocities to GitHub Pages because git's honestly way easier to sync files with.
  • Moving the site's content from pure HTML pages to Markdown documents via the Hugo framework. This should make it significantly easier for me to add new stuff to the site! In the meantime, things may break or might not look 1:1 with the old site.
  • The art gallery has been totally reworked, and now uses a Hugo Shortcode Gallery to render images. This also makes it way easier to add new art, at the expense of some flexibility.
  • Also Added some art I forgot to add since March!
  • The History of BUDD and BUDD Characters pages are being updated to use Markdown and are somewhat broken at the moment. I also really need to get around to updating the history page with all the stuff that happened since SAGE '23 because a LOT has happened.
  • This isn't related to the site at all, but did you know that BUDD has a Steam page now? You should check it out and wishlist my game! Tell your friends to wishlist it too!
