Welcome to EngineerKappa's River!

Hi there! I'm Anna, also known as EngineerKappa, and occasionally QueenDelta.

I'm an artist, a game developer, and an all around creative weirdo that doesn't know how to focus on a single thing. I'm just doing what I can, and that leads me down all sorts of wacky creative rabbitholes. This is my little hub to gather my thoughts, talk about the things I've made, share things I like, and really just carving a small space out for myself on an increasingly hostile corporate-owned internet.

The navigation bar at the top has some of the more important pages, but I've got a few subpages lying around too.
