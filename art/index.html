Artwork

I'm a digital artist, for the most part. I've been drawing all my life, but I only got a tablet sometime around about 2011 or 2012? That's when I really got serious about improving my art skills. Right now I'm using an XP-Pen tablet and Clip Studio Paint to draw.

These are drawings I did starting from mid-2022. If you'd like to see more, check out my social media pages. You can find my art on Newgrounds, Deviantart, and Tumblr.

If you're interested in art commissions, please check out my Google form! I'm not always open, but the form page should let you know if I am.

Be sure to click each image to open the full size version! Most are fairly large, though.
